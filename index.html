Goal of Breakdown Notes

The goal is to help you make notes quickly, and reorder them as you get more information or different ideas. FAQ

Breakdown Notes works in most recent browsers, but works best in Google Chrome.

There is no profiling or scanning of your maps. We store no information about you.
